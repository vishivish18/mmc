'Above The Noise' was envisioned by Jimmon Joy as a platform for students to showcase their hidden talents, to increase their self-belief and discipline. Our students spend months preparing for the show. It is their opportunity to shine.

 

It offers unique experiences and opportunities, developing discipline, focus, accountability, self-esteem and a sense of accomplishment.
