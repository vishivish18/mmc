Learn About the Mentor

JIMMON JOY

 

Founder & Director at Maestro Music Club, Noida, Jimmon was introduced to music at the young age of 5. His interest in music ranges from classical to rock to fusion. Stumbled upon his flair for the "six strings" at the age of 16, Jimmon has been giving guitar lessons for the past 11 years. He is the lead guitarist for the gospel band - David's Harp. He has been associated with various fusion bands like Soch, Raagaveda, Five Pegs Down and also with Leela N'Trance , as their Drummer. Jimmon has performed at many live concerts with International Artists and judges various competitions.

DRUMS

Learn About the Mentor

ALLEN

Allen Thomas, also known as "Fire" for his ability to electrify the audience, is our 1st Drums faculty at Maestro Music Club. He is a certified Drummer and a topper from Trinity School of Music, London. He performs and tours as part of the band - Passages.

 

 

 

 

NEHUL

 

Nehul, our 2nd Drums faculty, is influenced by some of the greatest artists around the world, including Neil Pert, Mike Mangini, Thomas Lang, Mike Portnoy, Gavin Harrison, etc. He was introduced to music at an early age of 10. He teaches students with sheer focus to make them better drummers

Learn About the Mentor

SHIMANTO GHOSH

 

Shimanto is our instructor for Hindustani Vocals at Maestro Music Club. Being a trained classical vocalist, he is also into Fusion and Jazz music. He was, formerly, the lead vocalist of his band - Raagveda. He has performed in many gigs, and got accolades for his performances in various cities across India, and in events  like Raahgiri. Having put in a lot of hard-work in his own vocal training, he understands the need of a focused long-term training for proper development of any budding vocalist or musician.
